Greggs Vegan and Vegetarian Menu

Since the Vegan Sausage Roll launched in 2019, Greggs has steadily added more plant-based options across most menu categories. You can now find vegan choices in bakes, sweet treats, and drinks.

Current vegan options include:

  • Vegan Sausage Roll
  • Vegan Steak Bake

Greggs Three-Tier Pricing Explained

Greggs prices are not the same everywhere, and most people only find this out at the till. There are three distinct price tiers depending on which type of store you are buying from, and the difference between the cheapest and most expensive can be more than you expect.

Store Type Price Level Example: Sausage Roll
Standard High Street Base price £1.35
City and London Branches 11 to 19% higher Around £1.45/td>
Airport and Motorway Franchise 27 to 55% higher £1.85 or more

City centre branches pay higher rents, and franchise operators at airports and motorway services work under a separate pricing agreement. If you are at an airport and the price looks steep, that is why.

FAQs - Greggs Menu

  1. What is on the Greggs menu in 2026?
    The Greggs menu covers savouries and bakes, breakfast, sandwiches and salads, hot food, sweet treats, and drinks, plus pizza, vegan options, and seasonal limited-time items.
  2. How much does a Greggs Sausage Roll cost?
    A Sausage Roll costs £1.35 at a standard store, around £1.45 at a city or London branch, and £1.85 or more at a franchise or airport location.
  3. Does Greggs have vegan options?
    Yes, Greggs has a growing vegan range including the Vegan Sausage Roll, Vegan Steak Bake, and Vegan Sausage, Bean and Cheese Melt.
  4. What time does Greggs serve breakfast?
    Greggs serves breakfast from 6:30 AM until 11:00 AM in most UK branches.
  5. How much is the Greggs meal deal?
    The Classic Greggs Meal Deal costs £3.75 at standard stores. The Breakfast Deal is £3.25 and the Premium Meal Deal costs £4.85.
  6. Why is Greggs more expensive at airports?
    Greggs charges roughly 27 to 55 percent more at airport and motorway franchise locations compared with standard UK stores, mainly due to higher commercial rents and a separate franchise pricing agreement.
  7. Does Greggs have a loyalty program?
    Yes, Greggs Rewards lets you earn points on every purchase through the app, with free drinks and treats available once you reach certain thresholds.
  8. Can I order Greggs online or through delivery?
    Yes, you can order through the Greggs app for Click and Collect, and through delivery platforms like Just Eat and Uber Eats where available.
