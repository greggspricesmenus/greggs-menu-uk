Greggs Breakfast Menu

Greggs breakfast times run from 6:30 AM until 11:00 AM at most UK locations. Once that window closes, breakfast comes off the counter and the lunch menu takes its place, so getting there with time to spare matters if a bacon roll is what you came for.

Breakfast options include bacon rolls, sausage rolls, egg and bacon baguettes, and porridge. Pairing any roll or baguette with a hot drink through the Breakfast Deal at £3.25 saves money compared to buying them separately.
